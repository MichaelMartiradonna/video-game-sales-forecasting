# Video Game Sales Analysis & Forecasting

**Project Goal**  
Analyzed historical video game sales data for the online store Ice to identify patterns that determine a game's success and help plan future advertising campaigns.

**Key Technologies**  
- Python, Pandas, NumPy  
- Matplotlib & Seaborn (visualization)  
- SciPy (statistical hypothesis testing)

**Main Findings**  
- PS4 and Xbox One dominate global sales in recent years.  
- Action and Shooter genres perform best in North America and Europe.  
- Japan has very different preferences (Role-Playing and 3DS lead).  
- High critic scores strongly correlate with higher sales.

**Business Recommendation**  
Focus advertising budget on PS4 titles with strong critic scores in Western markets, and on Role-Playing games for the Japanese market.

Open the Jupyter notebook in Google Colab or Jupyter Notebook.
